What Does “Adopt, Don’t Shop” Mean?

When you decide to bring a new furry companion into your life, you are faced with a choice that might seem simple but carries profound implications. You can either buy a pet from a breeder or a pet store, or you can choose to adopt. This decision is what “Adopt, Don’t Shop” is all about.

The Meaning Behind “Adopt, Don’t Shop”

At its core, the meaning of “Adopt, Don’t Shop” is straightforward: it encourages potential pet owners to adopt their pets from shelters and rescue groups instead of purchasing them from breeders or pet stores.
